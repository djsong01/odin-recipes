Korean Spicy Rice Cake

Home

Description

Teokbokki is a popular Korean street food: spicy rice cakes simmered in a red chili sauce

Ingredients

  • 350g Korean rice cakes
  • 5 ounces Korean fish cakes
  • 2 cups of soup stock - dried kelp
  • 3 tablespoon gochujang
  • 1 tablespoon soy sauce
  • 2 tablespoon raw suger
  • 2 green onion, chopped
  • 1 tablespoon sesame oil
  • 1 garlic clove, minced

Steps

  1. Soak Rice Cakes: Soak rice cakes in warm water (10 mins).
  2. Boil Sauce: Boil soup stock, stir in tteokbokki sauce.
  3. Add Ingredients: Add rice cakes, fish cakes, and onion.
  4. Cook: Boil 3-5 mins until rice cakes are cooked.
  5. Thicken: Simmer 2-4 mins to thicken sauce.
