Description

Kalbi-jjim is a Korean braised short rib dish. It's characterized by tender, fall-off-the-bone beef ribs cooked in a savory-sweet sauce with vegetables like carrots, potatoes, and mushrooms. It's a rich, flavorful, and often festive dish.

Ingredients

  • 2 pounds Korean-style short ribs
  • 3/4 cups soy sauce
  • 3/4 cups brown sugar
  • 3/4 cups water
  • 2 green onion, chopped
  • 1 tablespoon sesame oil
  • 1 garlic clove, minced

Steps

  1. Make Marinade: Mix marinade ingredients.
  2. Marinate Ribs: Coat ribs, refrigerate 3 hours.
  3. Prep Grill: Preheat grill (medium-high).
  4. Grill Ribs: Grill 5-7 mins per side, until cooked.
