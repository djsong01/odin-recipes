Description

Gimbap is a Korean seaweed rice roll, filled with various ingredients like vegetables, eggs, and meats

Ingredients

  • 4 dried seaweed sheets
  • 4 ounces spinach
  • 2 eggs, beaten
  • 1 carrot
  • 4 imitation crab sticks
  • 1 Spam, sliced
  • 4 yellow radish pickles
  • 2 cups of rice
  • 2 tablespoon sesame oil

Alternative Ingredients

  • Replace spinach with cucumber
  • Replace spam with bulgogi
  • Add cheese
  • Add cream cheese

Steps

  1. Prep Spinach: Rinse, blanch (30-60 sec), drain, squeeze, season with salt and sesame oil.
  2. Cook Egg: Scramble egg, cook into thin omelette, slice into strips.
  3. Cook Fillings: Briefly saut√© carrots, crab sticks, and ham separately.
  4. Season Rice: Mix cooked rice with sesame oil and salt.
  5. Assemble Station: Gather all ingredients for easy rolling.
  6. Layer Rice: Spread rice thinly over 2/3 of seaweed sheet (shiny side down).
  7. Add Fillings: Place fillings (radish, burdock, ham, crab, egg, carrot, spinach) evenly.
  8. Roll: Roll tightly, moistening the top edge of the seaweed to seal.
  9. Repeat: Roll remaining kimbap.
  10. Slice & Serve: Brush rolls with sesame oil, slice into bite-sized pieces, and serve.
